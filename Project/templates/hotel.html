Our Luxurious Hotel

Ensured to fit your needs

Our hotel is designed around giving you an incredible experience with our animals as well as giving you the most relaxing nights of your life. Our hotel resort is packed with everything you could ever wish for, from marble heated bathroom floors to cloud-like beds for a good nights sleep. On top of that, you get an incredible view of our entire Zoo where you can sit back and view the animals. Never miss out on what the animals are doing - day or night - without having to leave the comfort of your room!

Luxurious Sleeping

For the best of sleep







With beds like ours, you are sure to be sleeping like a royal! We want your stay to be the best it possibly can, so we ensure that our beds are cleaned and quality tested after every use so you can rest easy after your long day. We ensure that our mattresses are as quality and comfortable as they come and replace them if they don't meet our standards. We all know about those hard hotel mattresses - but you won't find those here.

Expertly designed architecture

For beautiful sights all around







Our hotel was designed with the idea of wildlife in mind. It is designed to make you feel as if you are living outside in the wild while delivering a comfortable place to relax. They masterfully crafted our hotel to be an unforgettable experience for all that we know you will just love.

Unbelievable bathrooms

For those moments you need privacy







Our luxury bathrooms provide you with those much-needed moments of privacy in luxury comfort. We have designed our bathrooms with relaxation in mind by eliminating any possible source of annoyance or discomfort. With heated floors and mirrors, our bathrooms are like no other. Feel the warmth of the floor under your feet and forget about cold tile floors. Forget about steamed-up mirrors, because our mirrors have heating which eliminates this problem so you don't have to wipe it down every few minutes.

Incredible views

From all levels, for all people







Whether you are up close and personal or like spectating from afar, our hotel provides you the ability to see our animals at any time of day. You may want to be up next to a bear, or watch a giraffe from a balcony up high. Whichever suits you best, we offer you the ability to do both! Pick from a wide range of our hotel rooms at different floor levels so you can choose how you interact with the animals.
